How AthleShield AI Works
1
Player logs daily wellness (60 seconds)
Each player logs 6 metrics every morning — sleep hours, RPE from yesterday's session, muscle soreness, mental fatigue, training load days, and any pain. No app install needed. Works via mobile web or WhatsApp bot.
2
Data stored in Firebase
Logs are timestamped and stored securely in Firebase Firestore under each player's ID. A 7-day rolling history is maintained per player — this is what enables trend detection, not just point-in-time snapshots.
3
AI physiologist analyses the data
A Firebase function triggers on each new log. It assembles the player's 7-day history + today's metrics + their position into a structured prompt sent to the Claude API. The AI acts as a certified sports physiologist — detecting acute:chronic workload spikes, position-specific injury risks, and fatigue compounding.
4
Coach sees squad readiness dashboard
Before every session, the coach opens the dashboard. Red = reduce load significantly. Amber = monitor closely. Green = full intensity. Each flagged player shows the AI's specific recommendation — e.g. "40% load, avoid sprints, hamstring at risk."
5
AI-generated training plan (one tap)
The coach taps "Generate today's plan." The AI produces a full position-specific session plan with individual modifications for every red and amber player. The coach runs the session backed by data — not gut feeling.

The Problem — Ground Truth
"I have 22 players. I know when someone looks tired, but I don't know if they're about to get injured. I've lost 3 players to hamstring pulls this season alone."

Grassroots football coaches across India manage squads of 15–25 players with zero structured fatigue monitoring. Every decision on who trains hard, who rests, and who is injury-prone is made on pure gut feeling.
